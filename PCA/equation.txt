result1

\underset{w}{max}F(w, \lambda) = \underset{w}{max}(w^{T}XX^{T}w+\lambda(1-w^{T}w))\\

\frac{\partial{F}}{\partial{w_{i}}} = 2XX^{T}w_{i} - 2\lambda w_{i} = 0\\
XX^{T}w = \lambda w
